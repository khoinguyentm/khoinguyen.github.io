About Me

I'm currently working as a programmer and I have a background of UX/UI design when I was a college student. After programming a couple years, I decided to go back to UX/UI because I wanted a job that allows me to be more creative. At the same time, I wanted to solve real user's problems and and improve people's life by creating useful products.

Thanks to my background in UX/UI, I took my first step to be a professional UX/UI Designer by being a part of the University of California, Irvine Bootcamp. It was an intense six months but the challenges I faced was overcame by my non-stop hard work and research. In the end, it all paid off as I was able to graduate with a UX/UI Certificate.
